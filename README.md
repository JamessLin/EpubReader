# EpubReader

<img width="301" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/794ca665-11c3-4150-87be-1f25904174ec" />

A simple, no-fuss EPUB reader that runs entirely in your browser.

It automatically saves your reading progress locally, so you can always pick up exactly where you left off. There's no server involved—just drop your book in and start reading.

### Features
* **Browser-based:** No installs needed, runs anywhere.
* **Auto-save:** Remembers your place across sessions.
* **Private:** Your books stay on your device.
